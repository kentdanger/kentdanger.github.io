About Kent Danger

Jordan Pippins, professionally known as Kent Danger, is a 27-year-old American guitarist, singer-songwriter, and producer based in Los Angeles. Kent’s work takes many forms—often fusing alternative rock, lo-fi hip hop, and progressive R&B into a futuristic, genre-defying blend as a solo artist and lead guitarist of C4RP00L.
